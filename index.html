How this works

Libraries we know exist but haven't been scanned yet

To find libraries near you
Tap 📍 at the top of the screen to centre the map on your location.
To see what's on a library's shelf
Tap its pin to open it, then look at the inventory section. You'll see the most recent shelf photos and the book titles read from them.
To search all libraries
Tap 🔍 at the top of the screen and type a title or author. We'll show you every library where that book's been spotted recently, sorted by distance from you.
To add a new library
Tap ＋ at the top of the screen.
To add or update a photo of the library box
Open a library and tap the 📷 Add a photo of this library placeholder — or tap 🏡 Update photo at the bottom of the screen to replace an existing photo.
To add an inventory
Tap a pin to open the library, then tap "📷 Scan the shelf" and take a photo. We'll read the spines automatically — you can fix anything we got wrong before saving. For best results, step back to get the whole interior in frame — one photo of both shelves beats two close-ups.
Spotted something wrong with a library?
Use 🚩 on its page.
